RunningDino is a simple game. Your goal is to avoid obstacles.
The main method is in src folder in DinoGame file.
After you open the game the menu will pop up, you can select options with arrows on your keyborad and confirm choice with ENTER.
If you click play, you can jump with UP arrow.
While playing you can press "R" to restart, "ESC" to open menu or "P" to pause game.
Scoreboard will save, if you close the game, but you have to use menu "exit" option.
